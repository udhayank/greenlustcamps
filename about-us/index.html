Why we do, What we do?

With growing population, our world is becoming small. Nowadays, finding true natural resources is very difficult. Human beings by nature have tendency to get attracted by natural resource for healthy well being. On other side, there is need for exploiting natural resources by consumption, wasteages and also through technology advancement in the complete ecosystem. Greenlust preserves the natural environment and happy to provide our people the best experience through campsite in nature centric and pollution free environment.

Due to internet penetration and digital advancement, people have been tirelessly working and often get stressed. Escaping from the system is quite difficult. Stress is something which is unavoidable. However, one should know how to releive stress to get the balanced life. Greenlust is the most preferable place one will choose, when there is question of how to relieve stress. In our place, you are back to golden (olden) days to live in the place of greeneries, hear birds and other sounds of nature, free from noise pollution, fresh air to breathe, luxury comfort to feel, thrilling adventurous experience to enjoy and much more.

In order to bring traditional environmental values to human being, we provide pure organic home cooked delicious food in Sukoon Cafe of Greenlust. Today when the word ”Health” is uttered, the word “Medicine” follows. In Sukooon Cafe, we make you feel “Food is medicine and Medicine is not food”. Unhealthy food penetration is deep due to taste and marketing contents. Our unique value of Sukoon cafe is to provide tasty and healthy foods made from orgranic products.

We do things for Human Well-being and Longevity.
